<p><p>The Emergency Strobe Light Bar market includes two main types: Always On Mode and Strobe Mode. Always On Mode refers to lights that emit a constant, steady beam of light for continuous visibility. These are commonly used for emergency vehicles parked on the side of the road. On the other hand, Strobe Mode lights emit quick bursts of light at regular intervals, providing a more attention-grabbing effect. This mode is often preferred for vehicles in motion or during emergencies where visibility is crucial.</p></p>
