<p><ul><li>Slip Joint</li><li>Universal Joints</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive propeller shaft accessories market includes slip joint and universal joints. Slip joints allow the propeller shaft to lengthen and shorten as the vehicle moves, providing flexibility and reducing vibration. Universal joints connect two shafts at an angle, allowing for rotation and transfer of power between different components. Both types of accessories are essential for the smooth functioning of the propeller shaft in vehicles, ensuring efficient power transmission and overall vehicle performance.</p></p>
