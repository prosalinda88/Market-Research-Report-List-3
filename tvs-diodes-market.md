<p><ul><li>Uni-polar TVS</li><li>Bi-polar TVS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>TVS diodes are used in electronic devices to protect against voltage spikes and surges. Uni-polar TVS diodes are designed to protect against positive or negative voltage spikes, making them suitable for applications where only one polarity needs to be clamped. Bi-polar TVS diodes, on the other hand, can protect against voltage spikes in both positive and negative directions, offering more comprehensive protection. Both types of TVS diodes are essential in various industries such as automotive, telecommunications, and consumer electronics to ensure reliable operation and prevent damage to sensitive components.</p></p>
