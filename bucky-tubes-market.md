<p><p>Bucky tubes, also known as carbon nanotubes, are available in two main market types: single-wall and multi-wall. Single-wall Buckytubes consist of a single layer of carbon atoms arranged in a cylindrical structure, offering high strength and flexibility. Multi-wall Buckytubes, on the other hand, consist of multiple layers of carbon atoms stacked on top of each other, providing enhanced mechanical and electrical properties. Both types of Buckytubes have a wide range of applications in industries such as electronics, aerospace, and automotive.</p></p>
