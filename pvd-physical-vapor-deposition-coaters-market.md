<p><p>PVD (Physical Vapor Deposition) Coaters Market includes different types of equipment such as Evaporation Equipment, Sputtering Equipment, and others. Evaporation Equipment is used for depositing thin films by heating and evaporating a material inside a vacuum chamber. Sputtering Equipment involves bombarding the target material with high-energy particles to create a thin film. Other types of PVD coaters include ion plating, cathodic arc deposition, and magnetron sputtering. These different types cater to various industries requiring precise and efficient coating processes.</p></p>
