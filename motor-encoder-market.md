<p><strong>The Motor Encoder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Incremental Motor Encoder</li><li>Absolute Motor Encoder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Motor encoders are devices used to convert shaft rotation into digital signals for precise position control in various applications. Two main types are incremental motor encoders and absolute motor encoders. Incremental encoders generate a series of pulses for each shaft rotation, allowing for relative position measurement. Absolute encoders provide unique digital codes for each position, enabling absolute position feedback even after power loss. Both types have their own advantages and are widely used in industries such as automation, robotics, and CNC machines.</p></p>
