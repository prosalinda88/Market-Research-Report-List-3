<p><p>Filter paper for coffee comes in two main market types: non-heat-sealable and heat-sealable. Non-heat-sealable filter papers are generally used for disposable, single-use coffee filters. They are economical and easy to use, but they are not suitable for sealing purposes. Heat-sealable filter papers, on the other hand, are designed for sealing filter bags to keep coffee grounds secure during brewing. They provide a more professional and secure option for commercial coffee brewing applications.</p></p>
