<p><p>Copperplate printing ink is available in various types to suit different printing needs. Solvent-borne ink is solvent-based and evaporates to dry, making it suitable for fast-drying applications. Water-borne ink is environmentally friendly and uses water as a base, making it non-toxic and easy to clean up. Oil-based ink is made from oils and resins, providing rich colors and a glossy finish. Each type of ink offers unique benefits and is chosen based on the specific requirements of the printing job.</p></p>
