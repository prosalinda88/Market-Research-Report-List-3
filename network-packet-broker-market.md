<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Network Packet Broker Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inline Network Packet Broker</li><li>Non-inline Network Packet Broker</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Inline Network Packet Brokers sit directly in the network traffic flow, allowing for real-time processing and redirection of packets. They offer advanced capabilities such as load balancing, packet filtering, and deep packet inspection. Non-inline Network Packet Brokers operate out-of-band, meaning they are connected to a network tap or mirror port instead of directly in the traffic flow. They provide packet aggregation, filtering, and distribution capabilities without impacting network performance. Both types play a crucial role in optimizing network visibility and security.</p></p>
