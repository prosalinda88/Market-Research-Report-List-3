<p><p>The Ceramic Positive Temperature Coefficient (PTC) Thermistors market includes two main types: Self Heating Mode and Sensor Mode. In the Self Heating Mode, the PTC thermistor acts as a self-regulating heater, where its resistance increases with temperature, providing a built-in heating element. On the other hand, in the Sensor Mode, the PTC thermistor is used as a temperature sensor, where its resistance changes with temperature variations to accurately measure and monitor temperature levels in various applications. Both modes have diverse applications in industries such as automotive, electronics, and healthcare.</p></p>
