<p><strong>The Infrared Fluorescent Ink Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Infrared Excited Ink</li><li>Infrared Absorption Ink</li><li>Infrared Covering Ink</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Infrared Fluorescent Ink market consists of three main types: Infrared Excited Ink, Infrared Absorption Ink, and Infrared Covering Ink. Infrared Excited Ink emits fluorescence when excited by an infrared source, making it suitable for security and authentication applications. Infrared Absorption Ink absorbs infrared light, making it invisible under infrared detection, ideal for covert markings. Infrared Covering Ink is designed to cover existing markings and images, rendering them visible only under infrared light, useful for anti-counterfeiting measures.</p></p>
