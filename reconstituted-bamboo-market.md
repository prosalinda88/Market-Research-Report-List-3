<p><p>Reconstituted bamboo is processed in two main ways: cold pressing and hot pressing. Cold pressing involves compressing bamboo fibers at room temperature, resulting in a softer and more pliable material suitable for products like flooring and furniture. Hot pressing, on the other hand, involves applying heat and pressure to create a harder and more durable material used for applications like construction materials and industrial products. Both markets cater to different needs, with cold pressing offering flexibility and hot pressing providing strength and longevity.</p></p>
