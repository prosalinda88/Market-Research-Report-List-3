<p><p>The Automobile Anti-Theft System market consists of several types of security devices to protect vehicles from theft. Steering locks physically prevent the steering wheel from being turned. Alarms sound loud alerts when unauthorized access is detected. Immobilizers disable the engine until a unique key or code is used. Remote keyless entry allows for easy and secure locking and unlocking of the vehicle. Central locking systems secure all doors simultaneously. Biometric capture devices use unique biological features for secure access to the vehicle.</p></p>
