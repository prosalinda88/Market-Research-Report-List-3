<p><strong>The Electric Vehicles BMS Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Distributed</li><li>Centralized</li><li>Modular</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electric Vehicles Battery Management System (BMS) market offers different types of systems to manage the battery performance. Distributed BMS involves individual control units for each battery cell, providing precise monitoring and protection. Centralized BMS utilizes a single control unit for the entire battery pack, simplifying the system but compromising on accuracy. Modular BMS combines both distributed and centralized approaches, offering flexibility and scalability for different vehicle setups. Each type caters to specific requirements and preferences of electric vehicle manufacturers and users.</p></p>
