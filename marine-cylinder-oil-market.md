<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Marine Cylinder Oil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High-BN (70-100 BN)</li><li>Low-BN (15-60 BN)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Marine cylinder oils can be categorized into two main types based on their Base Number (BN) levels: High-BN (70-100 BN) and Low-BN (15-60 BN). High-BN oils are designed for use in engines operating at high temperatures and have excellent neutralizing capabilities to combat acidic combustion products. On the other hand, Low-BN oils are suitable for engines running at lower temperatures and have lower alkalinity to prevent excessive alkaline residues. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring the smooth and efficient operation of marine engines.</p></p>
