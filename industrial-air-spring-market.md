<p><p>Industrial air springs come in various types, such as convoluted, sleeves, and others. Convoluted air springs have a bellows-like shape which allows for greater deflection and flexibility. Sleeves are cylindrical in shape and provide a smooth, even force over a larger surface area. Other types of air springs may include rolling lobe, taper, or multi-convoluted designs, each offering unique benefits for different industrial applications. Despite their differences, all these air springs serve the common purpose of providing cushioning and vibration isolation in industrial machinery.</p></p>
