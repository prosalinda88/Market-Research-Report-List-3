<p><strong>The Piezoelectric Motors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>AC Piezoelectric Motors</li><li>DC Piezoelectric Motors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Piezoelectric motors are classified into two main types - AC Piezoelectric Motors and DC Piezoelectric Motors. AC Piezoelectric Motors operate on an alternating current power source, which causes the piezoelectric material to expand and contract, resulting in rotational motion. On the other hand, DC Piezoelectric Motors operate on a direct current power source and utilize the piezoelectric effect to generate linear motion. Both types of motors are used in various applications such as robotics, aerospace, and medical devices for their precision and fast response capabilities.</p></p>
