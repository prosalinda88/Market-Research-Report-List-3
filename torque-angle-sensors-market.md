<p><p>Torque angle sensors are used in various industrial applications to measure the rotational angle and torque of a shaft or component. There are two main types of torque angle sensors in the market: contact type and non-contact type. Contact type sensors require physical contact with the rotating shaft to measure the torque and angle, while non-contact type sensors use magnetic or optical technologies to measure these parameters without the need for direct contact. Both types offer accurate and reliable measurement capabilities for different industrial needs.</p></p>
