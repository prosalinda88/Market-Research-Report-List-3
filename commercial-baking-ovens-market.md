<p><ul><li>Convection Ovens</li><li>Deck Ovens</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Commercial baking ovens market is segmented into two main types: Convection ovens and Deck ovens. Convection ovens use fans to circulate hot air evenly, resulting in faster and more uniform cooking. They are ideal for baking breads, pastries, and meats. On the other hand, Deck ovens have stone or steel decks that retain and distribute heat evenly, producing crispy crusts and moist interiors. They are commonly used for baking pizzas, cookies, and artisan breads. Both types of ovens cater to different baking requirements and preferences in the commercial baking industry.</p></p>
