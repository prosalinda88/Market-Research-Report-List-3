<p><p>Thick film resistors are available in two main market types: SMD Type and Through Hole Type. SMD (Surface Mount Device) Type resistors are smaller in size and designed for automated assembly on printed circuit boards, making them popular for portable electronic devices. Through Hole Type resistors are larger and require manual soldering onto the circuit board, making them suitable for applications requiring higher power handling. Both types offer high reliability and performance, catering to different needs in the electronics industry.</p></p>
