<p><p>Medicine packaging adhesives are available in different types to meet diverse industry needs. Solvent adhesives use organic solvents for application and are known for their strong bonding capabilities. Solvent-free adhesives do not contain any solvents, making them a more environmentally friendly option. Waterborne adhesives are made using water as a base, offering good adhesion properties while being non-toxic. These different types cater to various requirements in the medicine packaging industry, providing choices for manufacturers based on their preferences and needs.</p></p>
