<p><p>One-handed keyboards come in two main types: mechanical keyboards and membrane keyboards. Mechanical keyboards are popular among gamers and professionals for their durability, tactile feedback, and satisfying key presses. They are known for their high price point but offer a premium typing experience. On the other hand, membrane keyboards are more affordable and commonly found in budget-friendly options. They use a rubber dome mechanism to register key presses, making them quieter and softer to type on compared to mechanical keyboards.</p></p>
