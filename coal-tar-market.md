<p><p>Coal tar is a byproduct of coal processing, and it is categorized into different types based on the temperature at which it is obtained. High temperature coal tar is produced at temperatures above 1000°C and is used for producing specialty chemicals. Medium temperature coal tar is obtained at temperatures between 700-1000°C and is commonly used for roofing materials. Low temperature coal tar, produced at temperatures below 700°C, is used for producing carbon materials. Each type of coal tar has specific applications based on its properties and composition.</p></p>
