<p><p>Heat shrink tubing is available in various types including PTFE, FEP, PFA, ETFE, PET, and PEEK. PTFE tubing offers high temperature resistance and chemical inertness, making it ideal for harsh environments. FEP tubing is similar but offers better clarity and flexibility. PFA tubing is known for its high temperature resistance and excellent dielectric properties. ETFE tubing is resistant to chemicals and abrasion. PET tubing is economical and has good mechanical properties. PEEK tubing is known for its high temperature and chemical resistance.</p></p>
