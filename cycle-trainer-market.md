<p><p>Classic trainers are basic stationary bike stands that allow cyclists to ride their bikes indoors. They provide resistance through magnetic or fluid mechanisms. Smart trainers are more advanced, with electronic sensors that can communicate with training apps and simulate real-world conditions, adjusting resistance automatically. They also offer features like virtual routes, power measurement, and connectivity to devices. Both types cater to different needs and preferences, with classic trainers being more affordable and simple, while smart trainers provide a more interactive and immersive training experience.</p></p>
