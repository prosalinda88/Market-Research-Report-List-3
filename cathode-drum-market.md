<p><ul><li>Single-layer Cathode Drum</li><li>Composite Cathode Drum</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cathode drums are used in mass spectrometry applications for accurate measurements of ions. Single-layer cathode drums have a single layer of cathode material, providing a simpler design and lower cost. On the other hand, composite cathode drums consist of multiple layers of different materials, offering enhanced performance and durability. Single-layer cathode drums are suitable for basic ion detection, while composite cathode drums are more advanced and preferred for complex ion analysis and high-precision measurements in research and industrial settings.</p></p>
