<p><p>Animal organic fertilizers are divided into two main types based on their physical state: dry and liquid. Dry animal organic fertilizers are solid forms of organic matter, such as manure or compost, that are applied to the soil in their original form. Liquid animal organic fertilizers are liquid solutions or suspensions that are made by mixing water with organic materials like manure or fish emulsion. Both types of fertilizers are used to improve soil fertility and provide essential nutrients to plants in a natural and sustainable way.</p></p>
