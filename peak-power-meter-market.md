<p><p>Peak Power Meters are commonly available in two types of markets: Single Channel and Dual Channel. Single Channel meters are designed to measure and display peak power levels of a single signal at a time. They are suitable for simple applications where only one signal needs to be monitored. On the other hand, Dual Channel meters have the capability to measure and display peak power levels of two signals simultaneously. This makes them ideal for more complex applications that require monitoring and comparing two signals simultaneously.</p></p>
