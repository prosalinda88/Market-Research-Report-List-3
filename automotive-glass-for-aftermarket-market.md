<p><p>Automotive Glass for Aftermarket market consists of different types including laminated glass, tempered glass, and others. Laminated glass is comprised of a layer of polyvinyl butyral that holds the glass together upon impact, providing safety and preventing shattering. Tempered glass is heat-treated to increase strength and durability, shattering into small, less harmful pieces upon impact. Other types may include acoustic glass, tinted glass, and heated glass for various functionalities and safety features in vehicles.</p></p>
