<p><strong>The Spectral Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Infrared Light Sensor</li><li>Ambient Light Sensor</li><li>Sunlight Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Spectral sensor market includes three main types of sensors: Infrared light sensors, ambient light sensors, and sunlight sensors. Infrared light sensors detect infrared radiation emitted by objects, commonly used in night vision cameras. Ambient light sensors measure the light intensity in a room, adjusting device brightness accordingly. Sunlight sensors detect the intensity of sunlight, helping in adjusting display brightness on electronic devices. These sensors are widely used in various industries such as automotive, consumer electronics, and healthcare for efficient and accurate data collection.</p></p>
