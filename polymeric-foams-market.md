<p><strong>The Polymeric Foams Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Closed Cell Foams</li><li>Open Cell Foams</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polymeric foams are categorized into Closed Cell Foams and Open Cell Foams in the market based on their structure and properties. Closed Cell Foams have sealed cells that are not interconnected, providing excellent insulation and water resistance. On the other hand, Open Cell Foams have interconnected cells that allow for flexibility and breathability. Each type of foam has specific applications and benefits, with Closed Cell Foams being used in insulation and packaging, while Open Cell Foams are commonly seen in furniture and bedding products.</p></p>
