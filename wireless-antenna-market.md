<p><ul><li>UHF</li><li>VHF</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wireless antennas are available in two main types - UHF (Ultra High Frequency) and VHF (Very High Frequency). UHF antennas operate in the frequency range of 300 MHz to 3 GHz, making them suitable for long-range communication and digital TV signals. VHF antennas operate in the frequency range of 30 MHz to 300 MHz and are commonly used for FM radio and analog TV signals. Both UHF and VHF antennas play a crucial role in enhancing wireless communication networks and ensuring reliable signal transmission.</p></p>
