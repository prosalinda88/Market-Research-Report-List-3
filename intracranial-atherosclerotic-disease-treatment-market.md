<p><p>Intracranial Atherosclerotic Disease Treatment Market includes two main types of treatment options: Stent and PTA Balloon. Stents are small mesh tubes that are implanted in the narrowed blood vessels to help keep them open and improve blood flow to the brain. PTA Balloons, on the other hand, are balloons that are inflated in the narrowed blood vessels to widen them and facilitate better blood flow. Both treatments are minimally invasive procedures and are commonly used to alleviate symptoms of intracranial atherosclerotic disease.</p></p>
