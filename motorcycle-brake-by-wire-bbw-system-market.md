<p><ul><li>Single-channel</li><li>Multiple-channel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Brake by Wire (BBW) systems in motorcycles come in two main types: single-channel and multiple-channel. In a single-channel system, there is one central control unit that manages the braking functions for the entire motorcycle. On the other hand, in a multiple-channel system, there are separate control units for each brake, allowing for more precise control and potentially enhanced safety. Both types of systems offer advantages in terms of responsiveness and overall braking performance, catering to different preferences and needs of riders.</p></p>
