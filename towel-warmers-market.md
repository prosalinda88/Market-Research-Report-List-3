<p><p>Towel warmers are available in two main types, electric and hydronic. Electric towel warmers are powered by electricity and heat up quickly, providing efficient and convenient warming solutions. On the other hand, hydronic towel warmers use a liquid heating solution, such as hot water or oil, to heat up the towels. They are known for providing consistent and energy-efficient heating. Both types offer various designs and styles to suit different preferences and bathroom decor.</p></p>
