<p><ul><li>Rapidly Digestible Starch</li><li>Slowly Digestible Starch</li><li>Resistant Starch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Moulding starch can be classified into three market types based on their digestibility: rapidly digestible starch, slowly digestible starch, and resistant starch. Rapidly digestible starch is quickly broken down by the body, providing a fast source of energy. Slowly digestible starch is digested more gradually, providing a sustained release of energy. Resistant starch resists digestion in the small intestine, acting more like fiber and offering potential health benefits such as improved gut health and blood sugar management.</p></p>
