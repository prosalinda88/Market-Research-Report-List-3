<p><p>MEMS Air Velocity Sensor market consists of Mechanical Air Velocity Sensor and Ultrasonic Air Velocity Sensor types. Mechanical Air Velocity Sensors use moving parts to measure air flow while Ultrasonic Air Velocity Sensors use sound waves to determine air velocity. Both types have unique advantages; Mechanical sensors have high accuracy and reliability, whereas Ultrasonic sensors are non-intrusive and can measure flow in ducts with minimal disruption. The choice between the two depends on the specific application requirements and environmental factors.</p></p>
