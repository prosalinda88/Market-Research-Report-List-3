<p><ul><li>Single Pile Loop</li><li>Double Face Loop</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Terry Knitting Machine market includes two main types: Single Pile Loop and Double Face Loop. Single Pile Loop machines produce a looped fabric with one side having loops while the other side is flat. On the other hand, Double Face Loop machines create a fabric with loops on both sides, providing more thickness and warmth. Each market type caters to different needs and preferences, with Single Pile Loop being lighter and more breathable, and Double Face Loop offering more insulation and comfort.</p></p>
