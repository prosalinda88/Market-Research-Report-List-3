<p><p>Chemical Mechanical Polishing (CMP) pads are used in the semiconductor industry to achieve a smooth and flat surface on wafers. There are two main types of CMP pads: Hard CMP Pads and Soft CMP Pads. </p><p>Hard CMP Pads are designed for aggressive material removal and fast polishing rates, making them suitable for harder materials. Soft CMP Pads, on the other hand, provide gentle polishing with lower material removal rates, making them ideal for softer materials. Both types of pads have specific applications based on the material being polished and desired polishing results.</p></p>
