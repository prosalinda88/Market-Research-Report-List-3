<p><p>Wi-Fi MCU chips are commonly categorized into two types based on the frequency bands they support: Single Band and Dual Band. Single Band chips operate on the 2.4GHz frequency band, offering basic connectivity for everyday applications. On the other hand, Dual Band chips support both 2.4GHz and 5GHz frequency bands, providing faster speeds and better performance in crowded networks. Dual Band chips are ideal for high-demand applications such as streaming HD videos or online gaming.</p></p>
