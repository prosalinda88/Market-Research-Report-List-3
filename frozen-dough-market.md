<p><ul><li>Pre-fermented Frozen Dough</li><li>Pre-baked Frozen Dough</li><li>Unfermented Frozen Dough</li><li>Fully-baked Frozen Dough</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The frozen dough market can be categorized into four main types based on the dough's processing stage: pre-fermented frozen dough, pre-baked frozen dough, unfermented frozen dough, and fully-baked frozen dough. Pre-fermented frozen dough has undergone fermentation before freezing, pre-baked frozen dough is partially baked before freezing, unfermented frozen dough is frozen without any fermentation process, and fully-baked frozen dough is completely baked before freezing. Each type offers different advantages for consumers and businesses in terms of convenience, freshness, and customizability.</p></p>
