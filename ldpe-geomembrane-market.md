<p><strong>The LDPE Geomembrane Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Extrusion</li><li>Calendering</li><li>Blown Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>LDPE Geomembrane market types refer to the different methods used to manufacture LDPE geomembranes. Extrusion involves melting LDPE resin and pushing it through a die to form a continuous sheet. Calendering involves passing LDPE resin through a series of rollers to create a smooth and uniform sheet. Blown film market utilizes a similar process as extrusion but with additional air blown into the resin to form a thin film. Each method has its advantages and is suitable for different applications in the construction and environmental industries.</p></p>
