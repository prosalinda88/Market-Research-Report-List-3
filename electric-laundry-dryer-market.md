Heat Pump dryers use a heat exchange system to conserve energy and create a more efficient drying process. Condenser dryers collect moisture from clothes in a tank that needs to be emptied periodically. Vented dryers expel moist air through a hose or ventilation system. Each type has its own benefits and drawbacks, catering to different consumer needs and preferences in the laundry appliance market.</p></p>
