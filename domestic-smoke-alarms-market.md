<p><p>Domestic smoke alarms are crucial for early detection of fires in homes. There are two main types available in the market - ionisation type and photoelectric type. Ionisation alarms are more sensitive to flaming fires, while photoelectric alarms are better at detecting smouldering fires. Both types have their strengths and weaknesses, and it is recommended to have a combination of both types installed in a home for comprehensive fire detection and safety.</p></p>
