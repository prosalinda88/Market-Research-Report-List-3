<p><p>Latent curing agents are a type of curing agent used in the production of various coatings, adhesives, and composites. They are available in two main types: powder and liquid. Powder latent curing agents are finely ground solid particles that are typically mixed into the resin before application. Liquid latent curing agents, on the other hand, are pre-dissolved solutions that are easier to handle and mix with resins. Both types offer unique advantages and are chosen based on specific application requirements.</p></p>
