<p><p>Nano-silica market can be divided into two main types: P-type and S-type. P-type nano-silica is primarily used in the rubber industry for reinforcing properties, enhancing wear resistance, and improving mechanical strength. On the other hand, S-type nano-silica is widely employed in the construction industry for its excellent pozzolanic properties and ability to enhance the durability and strength of concrete. Both types play a crucial role in their respective industries, driving the growth of the nano-silica market.</p></p>
