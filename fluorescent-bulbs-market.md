<p><p>The market for Fluorescent bulbs is divided into two main types: Compact Fluorescent Bulbs (CFL) and Linear Fluorescent Bulbs (LFL). CFLs are small, energy-efficient bulbs that screw into standard light sockets and are commonly used in homes and offices. They provide bright, flicker-free light and are available in a variety of shapes and sizes. LFLs, on the other hand, are long, tube-shaped bulbs commonly used in commercial and industrial settings. They are known for their high efficiency and durability, making them popular choices for large spaces.</p></p>
