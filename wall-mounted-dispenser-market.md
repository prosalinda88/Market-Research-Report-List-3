<p><p>Wall-mounted dispensers come in two main types: manual and electric. Manual dispensers require physical effort to dispense products, making them more cost-effective and environmentally-friendly. They are commonly used in public restrooms for hand sanitizer or soap. On the other hand, electric dispensers are more convenient and hygienic as they dispense products without any physical contact. They are often found in high-traffic areas like hospitals or restaurants where quick and touch-free dispensing is a priority.</p></p>
