<p><p>Corrugated paper market consists of two main types: Single Corrugated Cardboard and Double Corrugated Cardboard. Single Corrugated Cardboard, also known as single wall corrugated, is made up of one layer of fluted paper glued between two flat sheets. It is commonly used for packaging light to medium-weight items. Double Corrugated Cardboard, or double wall corrugated, consists of three layers of paper - two fluted layers sandwiched between three flat sheets. This type is stronger and more durable, making it suitable for heavy or fragile items.</p></p>
