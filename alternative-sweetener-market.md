<p><p>The alternative sweetener market can be divided into two main types: Low-intensity sweeteners (LIS) and High-intensity sweeteners (HIS). LIS are sweeteners that provide a mild sweetness, making them suitable for products where a less intense sweet flavor is desired. HIS, on the other hand, are highly potent sweeteners that provide a strong sweetness with very little needed to achieve the desired taste. These two categories cater to different consumer preferences and are used in a wide range of food and beverage products.</p></p>
