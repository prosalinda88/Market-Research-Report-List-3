<p><p>Industrial laser cutting systems are classified into different types based on the cutting process. Laser vaporization cutting involves using high-energy lasers to evaporate material, resulting in precise cuts. Laser melting and cutting utilizes intense heat from the laser to melt and cut materials. Laser oxygen cutting involves using a focused beam of oxygen to create chemical reactions and cut through materials. Other types of laser cutting markets include fiber laser cutting, CO2 laser cutting, and UV laser cutting, each offering unique capabilities for specific applications.</p></p>
