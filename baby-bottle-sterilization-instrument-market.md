<p><p>Baby bottle sterilization instruments come in two main types: electric steam sterilization and microwave steam sterilization. Electric steam sterilization involves using a device that heats water to create steam, which then sterilizes the bottles. This method is convenient and effective, but may take longer than microwave sterilization. Microwave steam sterilization, on the other hand, involves placing the bottles in a special microwave-safe container with water and sterilizing them using the microwave's steam function. This method is quicker but may require more frequent cleaning of the microwave.</p></p>
