<p><p>Film photography cameras can be divided into two main market types: reusable film cameras and disposable film cameras. Reusable film cameras are designed for multiple uses, allowing photographers to replace the film and continue shooting. They are typically higher quality and offer more manual control options. On the other hand, disposable film cameras are one-time use cameras that are pre-loaded with film and then disposed of after the film is developed. They are more convenient and affordable for casual photographers or special occasions.</p></p>
