<p><p>Plastic clip bearings come in various types, including double flange bearings and split bearings. Double flange bearings provide extra support and stability due to the presence of flanges on both sides, reducing the risk of misalignment. Split bearings, on the other hand, are designed to be easily installed and removed by splitting into two halves, making maintenance tasks more convenient. Both types cater to different market needs and offer specific advantages depending on the application requirements.</p></p>
