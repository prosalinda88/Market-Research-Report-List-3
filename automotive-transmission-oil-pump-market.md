<p><p>Automotive transmission oil pumps are essential components that circulate oil to lubricate and cool transmission systems. There are two main types of oil pumps in the market: Mechanical oil pumps and Electric oil pumps. Mechanical oil pumps are driven by the engine's crankshaft, while Electric oil pumps are powered by the vehicle's electrical system. Mechanical oil pumps are traditional and reliable, while Electric oil pumps offer more efficiency and control. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring smooth operation and longevity of transmission systems in vehicles.</p></p>
