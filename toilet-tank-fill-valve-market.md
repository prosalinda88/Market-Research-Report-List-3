<p><p>The Toilet Tank Fill Valve market consists of two main types: Float Ball and Float Cup. In Float Ball systems, a ball attached to an arm rises with the water level in the tank, triggering the valve to shut off when the water reaches a certain level. Float Cup systems work similarly, with a cup replacing the ball to regulate the water level. Both types of fill valves are essential components in toilet tanks, helping to control the flow of water and ensure efficient filling and flushing of the toilet.</p></p>
