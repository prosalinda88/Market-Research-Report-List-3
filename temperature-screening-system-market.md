<p><ul><li>Mass Screening</li><li>Precise Screening</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Temperature screening systems are divided into two main market types: Mass Screening and Precise Screening. Mass Screening systems are designed for screening large groups of people quickly and efficiently, typically using non-contact infrared thermometers. These systems are ideal for high-traffic areas such as airports and shopping centers. Precise Screening systems, on the other hand, are more advanced and accurate, using technologies like thermal cameras and facial recognition. These systems are typically used in hospitals, laboratories, and other settings where precise temperature readings are crucial.</p></p>
