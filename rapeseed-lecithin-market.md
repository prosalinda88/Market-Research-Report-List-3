<p><p>Rapeseed Lecithin can be classified into two main types: Raw Rapeseed Lecithin and Refined Rapeseed Lecithin. Raw Rapeseed Lecithin is extracted directly from raw rapeseed without undergoing any further processing, making it more natural but with a stronger flavor. Refined Rapeseed Lecithin, on the other hand, is processed to remove impurities and enhance its taste and aroma, making it more suitable for food applications. Both types of Rapeseed Lecithin can be used as emulsifiers, stabilizers, and dispersants in various industries.</p></p>
