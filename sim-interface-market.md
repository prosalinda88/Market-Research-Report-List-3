<p><p>SIM interface market can be categorized into two main types: contact and contactless. Contact SIM cards require physical insertion into a device for data transfer and communication. On the other hand, contactless SIM cards use near-field communication technology to allow data transfer without the need for physical contact. Both types offer secure storage and communication of personal information, but contactless SIM cards provide a more convenient and efficient way of transferring data.</p></p>
