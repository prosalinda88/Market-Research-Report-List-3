The high voltage isolating switch is designed for applications with voltage levels above 1000 volts, while the low voltage isolating switch is suitable for voltage levels below 1000 volts. Both types of isolating switches provide a physical barrier between the electrical circuit and the person working on it, ensuring a safe working environment. Each type of isolating switch caters to specific voltage requirements to effectively isolate electrical circuits.</p></p>
