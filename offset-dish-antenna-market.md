<p><ul><li>Electronically Controlled Antenna (ESA)</li><li>Mechanically Controlled Antenna (MSA)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Offset dish antennas come in two main types: Electronically Controlled Antennas (ESA) and Mechanically Controlled Antennas (MSA). ESA utilizes electronic components to adjust the antenna's position for optimal signal reception, offering precise control and quick adjustments. On the other hand, MSA relies on mechanical systems like motors and gears to adjust the antenna, providing a more cost-effective solution while still delivering reliable performance. Both types cater to different market needs and preferences in the offset dish antenna industry.</p></p>
