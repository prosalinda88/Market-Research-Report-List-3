<p><p>The Electric Vehicle Regenerative Braking System market can be categorized into Electric, Hydraulic, and Other types. Electric regenerative braking systems use electric motors to slow down the vehicle and convert kinetic energy into electrical energy to recharge the battery. Hydraulic regenerative braking systems use hydraulic fluids to absorb and convert the vehicle's kinetic energy into potential energy. Other types of regenerative braking systems may include flywheel, electromagnetic, or mechanical systems that capture and store energy during braking to increase overall efficiency and reduce energy consumption in electric vehicles.</p></p>
