<p><p>Filter film capacitors are used to filter out unwanted signals or noise in electronic circuits. DC filter capacitors are designed to filter out DC noise or voltage fluctuations, while AC filter capacitors are intended for filtering out AC noise or ripple voltage. DC filter capacitors are commonly used in power supplies and DC circuits, while AC filter capacitors are used in applications such as audio equipment and motor drives. Both types of capacitors help improve the performance and reliability of electronic systems by providing a clean and stable power supply.</p></p>
