<p><p>The Aircraft Circuit Breaker Market is categorized based on the type of current they control - AC Type or DC Type. AC Type circuit breakers are designed to handle alternating current, while DC Type circuit breakers are designed for direct current. AC Type circuit breakers are commonly used in commercial aircraft systems that operate on AC power, while DC Type circuit breakers are more common in smaller aircraft or systems that run on DC power. Both types play a crucial role in protecting aircraft electrical systems from overload or short circuit events.</p></p>
