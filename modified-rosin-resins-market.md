<p><strong>The Modified Rosin Resins Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Maleic Modified Rosin Resins</li><li>Fumaric Modified Rosin Resins</li><li>Phenolic Modified Rosin Resins</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Modified Rosin Resins are classified into three types based on the type of modification they undergo. Maleic Modified Rosin Resins are modified using maleic anhydride, providing enhanced adhesion and resistance to chemicals. Fumaric Modified Rosin Resins are modified using fumaric acid, offering improved flexibility and compatibility. Phenolic Modified Rosin Resins are modified with phenolic compounds, resulting in enhanced heat resistance and durability. Each type caters to specific applications and industries, providing unique properties and benefits tailored to different needs.</p></p>
