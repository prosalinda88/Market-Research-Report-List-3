<p><strong>The Ultrahigh Strength Steel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dual Phase</li><li>Complex Phase</li><li>Multiphase</li><li>Martensitic</li><li>Transformation-Induced Plasticity</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ultrahigh strength steel market is segmented into various types based on microstructure and properties. Dual Phase steel combines soft ferrite and hard martensite phases for superior strength and ductility. Complex Phase steel contains multiple phases for high strength and formability. Multiphase steel offers a combination of phases for improved mechanical properties. Martensitic steel is hardened through heat treatment for increased strength. Transformation-Induced Plasticity steel undergoes transformation during deformation for enhanced ductility. Other types include TRIP, TWIP, and Q&P steel with unique properties.</p></p>
