<p><p>Automotive air filters play a crucial role in maintaining air quality inside vehicles. There are three main types of automotive air filters on the market: particle filters, activated carbon filters, and electrostatic filters. Particle filters are designed to capture dust, dirt, and other particles from the air. Activated carbon filters are effective in trapping organic compounds and odors. Electrostatic filters use an electric charge to attract and capture particles in the air. Each type of filter offers unique benefits in improving air quality and ensuring a healthier driving environment.</p></p>
