<p><p>Fire rated cables are designed to resist the spread of fire and maintain functionality during a fire emergency. There are two main types in the market: Single Core Fire Rated Cables and Multicore Fire Rated Cables. Single core cables have one central conductor, while multicore cables have multiple conductors within the same cable sheath. Single core cables are ideal for applications where a single power source is needed, while multicore cables are suitable for complex systems requiring multiple connections. Both types provide crucial fire safety features for various industries.</p></p>
