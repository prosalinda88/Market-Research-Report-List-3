<p><p>Sweet potato starch is available in two main market types: Fresh Type and Dried Type. </p><p>Fresh Type sweet potato starch is produced directly from freshly harvested sweet potatoes through a process of washing, crushing, and separating the starch from the pulp. This type of starch is commonly used in dishes requiring a smooth texture, such as soups and sauces.</p><p>Dried Type sweet potato starch is produced by drying the starch extracted from sweet potatoes. This type of starch has a longer shelf life and is often used as a thickening agent in various culinary applications.</p></p>
