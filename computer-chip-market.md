<p><p>The computer chip market is divided into two main types: Southbridge and Northbridge. Southbridge chips manage the communication between the CPU and external devices like hard drives and USB ports. Northbridge chips handle the communication between the CPU and memory, as well as the graphics card. Southbridge chips are typically slower but more versatile, while Northbridge chips are faster but have more limited functionality. Both types are essential for the overall performance and functionality of a computer system.</p></p>
