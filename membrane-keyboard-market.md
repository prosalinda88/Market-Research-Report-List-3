<p><p>Membrane keyboards are categorized into two types - flat-panel membrane keyboards and full-travel membrane keyboards. Flat-panel membrane keyboards have a sleek design with minimal tactile feedback, making them popular for their low-profile and quiet operation. On the other hand, full-travel membrane keyboards have keys that travel fully when pressed, providing a more traditional typing feel. Both types cater to different preferences and use cases in the market, offering a variety of options for consumers to choose from based on their needs and preferences.</p></p>
