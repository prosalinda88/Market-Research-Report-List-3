<p><p>Small and Medium Business (SMB) software market types include on-premise and cloud-based solutions. On-premise software is installed and run on the company's own computers and servers, providing direct control over the data and infrastructure. Cloud-based software, on the other hand, is hosted remotely on the vendor's servers and accessed over the internet, offering flexibility, scalability, and cost-effectiveness. Both options cater to the specific needs and preferences of SMBs, with on-premise solutions focusing on security and control, while cloud-based solutions emphasize accessibility and convenience.</p></p>
