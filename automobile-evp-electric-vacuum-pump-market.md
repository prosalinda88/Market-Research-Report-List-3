<p><p>The Automobile EVP (Electric Vacuum Pump) market consists of three main types: Diaphragm Type, Leaf Type, and Swing Pistol Type. </p><p>Diaphragm Type pumps use a flexible diaphragm to create vacuum pressure. Leaf Type pumps utilize a series of leafs to generate vacuum. Swing Pistol Type pumps use a swinging piston mechanism to create suction. Each type has its own advantages and applications in the automotive industry, catering to different needs and requirements of vehicles.</p></p>
