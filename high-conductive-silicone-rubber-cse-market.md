<p><p>Highly conductive silicone rubber (CSE) can be found in various market types such as compression molding, extrusion, injection molding, and other markets. Compression molding involves placing the silicone rubber in a mold and applying pressure and heat to create the desired shape. Extrusion involves forcing the material through a die to create long shapes like tubing or wire insulation. Injection molding uses a mold and injects the material into it under high pressure to form intricate shapes. Other markets may include applications such as coating, encapsulation, or gasketing.</p></p>
