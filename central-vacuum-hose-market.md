<p><p>Central vacuum hoses are available in three main types: dual voltage, low voltage, and no voltage hoses. Dual voltage hoses are designed to work with both standard electrical outlets and low-voltage systems. Low voltage hoses are specifically made for use with low-voltage central vacuum systems. No voltage hoses, on the other hand, do not require any electrical connection and are powered solely by suction. Each type caters to different power requirements and configurations, offering flexibility and convenience to users.</p></p>
