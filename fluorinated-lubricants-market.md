<p><p>Fluorinated lubricants come in various forms including oils, waxes, and greases. Fluorinated oils are liquid lubricants that provide thin film lubrication and are commonly used in high-speed applications. Fluorinated waxes are solid lubricants that offer excellent resistance to heat and chemicals, making them suitable for extreme conditions. Fluorinated greases are semi-solid lubricants that provide long-lasting lubrication in high load situations. Each type of fluorinated lubricant caters to specific needs in various industries such as automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing.</p></p>
