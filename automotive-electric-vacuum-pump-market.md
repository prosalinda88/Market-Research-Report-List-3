<p><p>Automotive Electric Vacuum Pump market consists of three main types - Diaphragm Type, Leaf Type, and Swing Piston Type. Diaphragm Type pumps utilize a flexible diaphragm to create a vacuum. Leaf Type pumps use rotating blades to create suction. Swing Piston Type pumps consist of a swinging piston that moves back and forth to generate vacuum pressure. Each type has its own benefits and applications in the automotive industry, catering to different needs and requirements of vehicles.</p></p>
