<p><p>Deep-groove ball bearings are classified into three main types based on the number of rows of bearing balls they contain. Single-row bearings have one row of balls, making them ideal for light to medium-duty applications. Double-row bearings have two rows of balls, providing increased load capacity and stiffness. Multi-row bearings have three or more rows of balls, offering even higher load-carrying capacity. Each type caters to various industrial and automotive application needs, providing manufacturers with a wide range of options to choose from.</p></p>
