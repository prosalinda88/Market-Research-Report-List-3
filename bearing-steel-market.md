<p><p>Bearing steel is primarily used in the manufacturing of bearing components such as bearing rings, rolling bodies, cages, and other parts. Bearing rings are the outer and inner rings that hold the rolling elements in place. Rolling bodies, typically in the form of balls or rollers, facilitate smooth rotation within the bearing assembly. Cages help to maintain the spacing and alignment of the rolling elements. Other parts include seals, shields, and lubrication systems that contribute to the overall performance of bearings.</p></p>
