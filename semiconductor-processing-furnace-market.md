<p><p>Semiconductor processing furnaces are used in the manufacturing of electronic components. Horizontal furnaces are designed for batch processing of wafers by placing them horizontally, allowing for uniform temperature distribution. Vertical furnaces, on the other hand, process wafers in a vertical orientation, providing better control over gas flow and temperature distribution. Both types of furnaces are essential in the semiconductor industry for deposition, annealing, and diffusion processes to create high-quality electronic devices.</p></p>
