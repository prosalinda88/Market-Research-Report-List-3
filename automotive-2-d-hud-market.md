<p><ul><li>Combiner</li><li>Windshield</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive 2D HUD (Head-Up Display) technology is typically divided into two main types: Combiner and Windshield HUD. Combiner HUDs project information onto a separate transparent screen that is mounted on the dashboard, while Windshield HUDs project data directly onto the windshield. Combiner HUDs are more common in older vehicles and provide a cost-effective solution, while Windshield HUDs offer a more integrated and futuristic display. Both types of HUDs aim to provide drivers with essential information such as speed, navigation directions, and other important alerts without requiring them to take their eyes off the road.</p></p>
