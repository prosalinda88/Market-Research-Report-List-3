These modules can be categorized into two types: Module and SoC (System on Chip). Module-based V2X systems include separate components for different functionalities, while SoC-based systems integrate all functionalities into a single chip. The Module market focuses on flexibility and scalability, while the SoC market emphasizes compactness and efficiency. Both types are crucial in advancing the development of connected and autonomous vehicles.</p></p>
