<p><p>Hydro turbine generator units market is categorized into various types such as Francis, Kaplan, Pelton, and others. Francis turbines are versatile and suitable for medium to high head applications. Kaplan turbines are ideal for low head and high flow applications. Pelton turbines are most effective in high head and low flow conditions. Other types include crossflow and axial turbines. Each type has its own unique design and operational characteristics suited to different hydroelectric power generation settings.</p></p>
