<p><ul><li>Plated Type</li><li>Brazed Type</li><li>Sintered Type</li><li>CVD Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Chemical Mechanical Polishing (CMP) Diamond Pad Regulator Market offers four main types: Plated Type, Brazed Type, Sintered Type, and CVD Type. Plated Type regulators are made by coating a base material with a layer of diamond. Brazed Type regulators are created by fusing diamond particles onto a base material. Sintered Type regulators are formed by pressing diamond particles together at high heat and pressure. CVD Type regulators are produced using chemical vapor deposition to grow a diamond layer on a substrate. Each type has specific advantages and applications in the CMP industry.</p></p>
