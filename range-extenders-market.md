<p><p>Range extenders are devices that are used to increase the coverage of an existing Wi-Fi network by amplifying the signal strength. They are an ideal solution for extending Wi-Fi coverage in areas where the signal is weak or nonexistent. Range extenders work by receiving the existing signal from a Wi-Fi router, amplifying it, and then broadcasting the boosted signal to areas that were previously out of range.</p><p>The range extenders market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 14.6% during the forecast period. The growth can be attributed to the increasing demand for improved Wi-Fi coverage in homes, offices, and public spaces. Additionally, the proliferation of smart devices and IoT technology has also contributed to the growth of the range extenders market, as users look for ways to ensure seamless connectivity for their devices.</p><p>Some of the latest trends in the range extenders market include the introduction of mesh Wi-Fi systems, which offer better coverage and reliability compared to traditional range extenders. Manufacturers are also focusing on developing range extenders with advanced features such as beamforming technology, multiple antennas, and easy setup options. Overall, the range extenders market is expected to continue to grow as the demand for better Wi-Fi coverage increases.</p></p>
