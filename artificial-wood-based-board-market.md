<p><p>Artificial wood-based boards are a common alternative to natural wood for various applications due to their cost-effectiveness and sustainability. The market includes different types such as Particle Board, Plywood, OSB (Oriented Strand Board), MDF/HDF (Medium-Density Fiberboard/High-Density Fiberboard), Hardboard, and others. Particle Board is made from wood particles bonded together, plywood consists of thin layers of wood veneer glued together, OSB is made from compressed wood strands, and MDF/HDF is composed of wood fibers bonded under high pressure. Each type offers unique qualities and is used in different industries.</p></p>
