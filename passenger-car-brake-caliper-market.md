<p><strong>The Passenger Car Brake Caliper Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Piston Caliper</li><li>Multi-Piston Caliper</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Passenger car brake calipers can be categorized into two main types: single piston calipers and multi-piston calipers. Single piston calipers have one hydraulic piston pushing the brake pads onto the rotor, providing braking force. On the other hand, multi-piston calipers have multiple pistons on each side of the caliper, distributing pressure more evenly across the brake pads for improved braking performance. Both types of calipers are essential components in the braking system of passenger cars, ensuring safe and efficient stopping power.</p></p>
