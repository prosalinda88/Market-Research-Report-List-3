<p><p>Natural gelling agents are commonly used in food and personal care products to provide texture and stability. Xanthan gum, gellan gum, guar gum, pectin, and gelatin are some of the popular types of gelling agents. Xanthan gum is a versatile thickener and stabilizer, while gellan gum is widely used in the food industry for gelling and suspending. Guar gum is a natural thickening agent derived from guar beans, and pectin is a plant-based gelling agent commonly used in jams and jellies. Gelatin, derived from animal collagen, is a widely used gelling agent in the food industry.</p></p>
