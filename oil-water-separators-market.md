<p><p>Oil water separators are classified into different types based on the type of oil that needs to be separated. These types include Mineral Oil, Synthetic Oil, POE (Polyolester), PAG (Polyalkylene Glycol), and Others. Mineral oil separators are used for separating traditional petroleum-based oils, while synthetic oil separators are designed for separating oils like silicone or ester-based synthetic oils. POE and PAG separators are specifically used for separating these particular types of oils. The "Others" category includes separators designed for specialty oils that do not fall into the other categories.</p></p>
