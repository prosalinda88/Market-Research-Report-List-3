<p><p>Polyoxymethylene (POM) plastic is available in two main market types: homo-polyoxymethylene and co-polyoxymethylene. Homo-polyoxymethylene, also known as homopolymer, is a type of POM plastic made from a single monomer unit. It offers high strength and stiffness, making it suitable for engineering applications. On the other hand, co-polyoxymethylene, or copolymer, is made from two different monomer units. It provides better impact resistance and flexibility compared to homo-polyoxymethylene, making it ideal for more demanding applications.</p></p>
