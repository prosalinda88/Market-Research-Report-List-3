<p><p>Polyamide block copolymers are used in various markets, including Injection Molding, Blow Molding, Thermoforming, and Extrusion Molding. Injection Molding involves injecting molten polymer into a mold to create a specific shape. Blow Molding utilizes air pressure to expand the polymer into a mold cavity. Thermoforming heats the polymer sheet and then molds it into a specific shape. Extrusion Molding involves forcing molten polymer through a die to create a continuous profile. Each market type offers unique advantages and applications for polyamide block copolymers.</p></p>
