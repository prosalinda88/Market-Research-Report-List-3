<p><p>The plastic knife market is categorized into different types based on the material used for manufacturing. These types include PET, PLA, PP, and others. PET knives are made from polyethylene terephthalate, which is a durable and lightweight plastic. PLA knives are biodegradable and made from polylactic acid. PP knives are made from polypropylene, known for their flexibility and heat resistance. Other types of plastic knives may include materials like polystyrene or PVC. Each type offers unique benefits and applications in the market.</p></p>
