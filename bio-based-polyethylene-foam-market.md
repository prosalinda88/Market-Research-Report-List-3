<p><strong>The Bio-based Polyethylene Foam Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sugar Cane Sourced</li><li>Other Sourced</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bio-based polyethylene foam is categorized into two types based on the source of raw materials used for production: Sugar cane sourced and other sourced. Sugar cane sourced bio-based polyethylene foam is made from ethylene produced through the fermentation of sugar cane. On the other hand, other sourced bio-based polyethylene foam is made from ethylene derived from sources other than sugar cane, such as corn, wheat, or cellulose. Both types offer eco-friendly alternatives to traditional petroleum-based polyethylene foam products, promoting sustainability and reducing carbon footprint.</p></p>
