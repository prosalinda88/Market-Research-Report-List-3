<p><p>Hay desiccants are chemicals used to dry hay before baling to prevent mold growth. There are different types of hay desiccants available in the market, including sulfur-based desiccants, buffered propionic acid desiccants, and others. Sulfur-based desiccants release sulfur dioxide gas to inhibit mold growth, while buffered propionic acid desiccants lower the pH of hay to prevent spoilage. Other types of hay desiccants may include chemicals like acetic acid or citric acid that work to dry the hay effectively.</p></p>
