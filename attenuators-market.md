<p><p>Attenuators are electronic devices used to reduce the strength of a signal without causing distortion. Passive attenuators are simple devices that use resistors to decrease signal strength, while active attenuators employ additional components such as transistors to actively adjust signal levels. The passive attenuator market is characterized by cost-effectiveness and simplicity, while the active attenuator market offers greater flexibility and control over signal attenuation. Both types of attenuators play a crucial role in various industries that require precise signal management and control.</p></p>
