<p><p>EPDM waterproofing membrane is available in three main types based on thickness - 1mm, 1.2mm, and 1.5mm. The 1mm membrane is suitable for low traffic areas or as a base layer in multi-layer systems. The 1.2mm membrane offers enhanced durability and is ideal for medium traffic areas. The 1.5mm membrane provides maximum protection and is recommended for high traffic areas or areas with the potential for mechanical damage. Each type has specific applications based on the level of protection required for the surface being waterproofed.</p></p>
