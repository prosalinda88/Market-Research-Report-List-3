<p><p>Desalination chemicals market includes various types such as inhibitors, defoamers, and fungicides. Inhibitors are used to prevent corrosion and scale formation in desalination systems, while defoamers are used to reduce foam formation during the desalination process. Fungicides are important for preventing the growth of algae and bacteria in desalination plants. Each type of chemical plays a crucial role in maintaining the efficiency and longevity of desalination systems by addressing specific challenges in the water treatment process.</p></p>
