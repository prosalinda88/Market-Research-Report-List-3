<p><p>Car Exhaust System Market involves two main types: Single Exhaust System and Dual Exhaust System. Single Exhaust System consists of one pipe that collects exhaust gases from the engine and releases them through a single muffler and tailpipe. On the other hand, Dual Exhaust System includes two separate exhaust pipes that collect and release exhaust gases, often improving engine performance and producing a more aggressive sound. Both types cater to different consumer preferences based on desired performance, aesthetics, and budget considerations.</p></p>
