<p><p>Cylindrical photoelectric sensors are used in various industries for object detection and presence sensing. Diffuse type sensors have the emitter and receiver in the same housing, making them easy to install and use. Diffusion type sensors have a separate emitter and receiver, allowing for longer sensing distances and more accurate detection. Both types are commonly used in manufacturing, packaging, and automation applications to improve efficiency and safety. Their versatility and reliability make them essential components in industrial settings.</p></p>
