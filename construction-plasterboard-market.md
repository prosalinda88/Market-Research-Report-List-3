<p><p>Construction plasterboard comes in three main types: regular, moisture resistant, and fire resistant. Regular plasterboard is suitable for general use in most residential and commercial buildings. Moisture resistant plasterboard is designed for areas prone to high humidity, such as bathrooms and kitchens. Fire resistant plasterboard is ideal for buildings where fire safety is a concern, as it contains additives that increase its resistance to fire. Each type of plasterboard serves a specific purpose in construction, providing builders with options to meet different building requirements.</p></p>
