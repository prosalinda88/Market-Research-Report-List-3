<p><p>Smart Card and Security Chip market can be categorized into two main types - Contact and Contactless. Contact smart cards require physical insertion into a card reader for data transmission, providing increased security. On the other hand, contactless smart cards utilize radio frequency technology for communication, allowing for easier and faster transactions. Both types have their own advantages and are widely used in various industries such as banking, healthcare, and transportation for secure data storage and access control.</p></p>
