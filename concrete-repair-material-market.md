<p><p>Concrete repair materials are categorized into three main types:</p><p>1. Polymer-Modified Cementitious Mortar: These materials are a blend of cement, fine aggregates, polymers, and other additives. They offer high strength, durability, and bonding properties.</p><p>2. Epoxy-Based Material: Epoxy materials provide excellent adhesion to concrete surfaces and offer resistance to chemical attack, abrasion, and impact.</p><p>3. Others: This category includes various specialized repair materials such as acrylic-based, polyurethane-based, and calcium aluminate cement-based materials. Each type offers unique properties suited for different repair applications.</p></p>
