<p><ul><li>Surface Embroidery</li><li>Counted Embroidery</li><li>Needlepoint</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Embroidery market types encompass surface embroidery, counted embroidery, and needlepoint. Surface embroidery involves stitching patterns on top of fabric, such as crewel and silk shading. Counted embroidery uses a chart or grid to follow a specific pattern, like cross-stitch. Needlepoint is a form of embroidery done on a stiff canvas with yarn, creating intricate designs. Each market type caters to different preferences and techniques, offering a wide range of choices for embroidery enthusiasts to explore and enjoy.</p></p>
