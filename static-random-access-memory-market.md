<p><ul><li>NVSRAM</li><li>Asynchronous SRAM</li><li>Synchronous SRAM</li><li>Low Power SRAM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Static Random Access Memory (SRAM) is available in various market types such as NVSRAM, Asynchronous SRAM, Synchronous SRAM, and Low Power SRAM. NVSRAM offers non-volatility by using a small integrated backup power source. Asynchronous SRAM operates independently of the system clock and is commonly used in general-purpose applications. Synchronous SRAM, on the other hand, synchronizes data transfers with the system clock for higher performance. Low Power SRAM is designed to consume minimal power, making it ideal for battery-powered devices and other energy-efficient applications.</p></p>
