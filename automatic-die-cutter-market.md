<p><p>The automatic die-cutter market consists of two main types: rotary die cutter and flat bed die cutter. Rotary die cutters are highly efficient for high-speed production of intricate shapes and designs, commonly used in industries like label manufacturing and packaging. On the other hand, flat bed die cutters are ideal for cutting thicker materials and larger sheet sizes, making them suitable for industries like automotive and furniture manufacturing. Both types cater to different needs and preferences within the die-cutting industry.</p></p>
