<p><p>Drop-on-demand inkjet printers are classified into different types based on the technology used to eject ink droplets onto the paper. Thermal inkjet printers use heat to vaporize the ink and create droplets, Piezo inkjet printers use electrically charged crystals to create droplets, and Electrostatic inkjet printers use static electricity to eject droplets. Each type of printer offers unique advantages and caters to specific printing needs, making them popular choices in the consumer and industrial markets.</p></p>
