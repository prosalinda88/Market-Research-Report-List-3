<p><p>Mineral insulated heating cables are classified into three types based on their structure and design: Single Conductor, Double Conductor, and Others. Single Conductor cables consist of a single conductor surrounded by mineral insulation, providing heat in a compact manner. Double Conductor cables feature two conductors embedded in the insulation, allowing for higher power output. The Others category includes unique variations of mineral insulated heating cables that may have multiple conductors or alternative insulation materials. Each type offers specific benefits and applications in various heating scenarios.</p></p>
