<p><p>Agricultural Horizontal Vortex Water Pump market includes two main types - Closed Vortex Pump and Open Vortex Pump. Closed Vortex Pump features a sealed impeller design that prevents clogs and is suitable for transferring clean water. On the other hand, Open Vortex Pump has an open impeller design for handling liquids with high solid content, making it ideal for wastewater and slurry applications. Both types cater to different agricultural needs and offer efficient water transfer solutions.</p></p>
