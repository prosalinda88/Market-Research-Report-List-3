<p><p>Low-alcohol beer market types include limit fermentation and dealcoholization method. In limit fermentation, the production process is controlled to limit the alcohol content naturally produced during fermentation. On the other hand, the dealcoholization method involves removing alcohol from regular beer through techniques such as vacuum distillation or reverse osmosis. Both methods result in low-alcohol beer with reduced alcohol content, catering to consumers looking for a lighter drinking option without compromising on taste.</p></p>
