<p><p>DC Response Accelerometer market types are categorized based on the number of axes they measure. 1-Axis accelerometers measure acceleration along one specific axis, commonly used for vertical or horizontal measurements. 2-Axis accelerometers measure acceleration along two axes simultaneously, commonly used for pitch and roll measurements. 3-Axis accelerometers measure acceleration along all three axes (X, Y, Z), providing complete spatial orientation information. Each type offers different capabilities and applications depending on the specific requirements of the user.</p></p>
