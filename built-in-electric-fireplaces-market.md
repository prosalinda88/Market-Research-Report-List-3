<p><p>Built-in electric fireplaces come in various types, with two popular options being radiant electric fireplaces and vented electric fireplaces. Radiant electric fireplaces emit heat through the use of infrared technology, providing a cozy and comforting warmth. On the other hand, vented electric fireplaces are designed to mimic the look and feel of a traditional wood-burning fireplace, complete with a vent that can be installed to release heat and smoke outside. Both types offer convenience and aesthetic appeal for homeowners looking to add a fireplace to their living space.</p></p>
