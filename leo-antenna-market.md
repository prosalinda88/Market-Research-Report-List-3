<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The LEO Antenna Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active</li><li>Passive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Active LEO antennas are equipped with their own power source and electronic components to actively amplify, filter, or steer incoming signals, providing higher gain and better performance compared to passive antennas. Passive LEO antennas, on the other hand, do not require a power source and rely on natural energy sources to receive and transmit signals. While passive antennas are simpler and more cost-effective, active antennas offer superior functionality and can adapt to changing signal conditions more effectively.</p></p>
