<p><ul><li>Toggle MRAM</li><li>STT-MRAM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive Magnetoresistive Random Access Memory (MRAM) can be classified into two main types: Toggle MRAM and STT-MRAM. Toggle MRAM utilizes magnetic fields to store data, offering high speed and non-volatility. On the other hand, STT-MRAM uses spin transfer torque to write data, providing even faster speeds and lower power consumption. Both types of MRAM are being increasingly used in automotive applications due to their durability and resistance to harsh conditions, making them ideal for use in automotive control systems and infotainment systems.</p></p>
