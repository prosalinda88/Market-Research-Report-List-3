<p><p>Plastic biocides such as BIT, DCOIT, IPBC, TBT, TBZ, and OBPA are commonly used in various plastic products to prevent microbial growth and deterioration. BIT and DCOIT are effective against bacteria and fungi, while IPBC is used for wood-plastic composites. TBT and TBZ are typically used in marine applications to prevent fouling by algae and barnacles. OBPA is utilized in plastic films and polymers to protect against bacteria and mold. These biocides play a crucial role in extending the lifespan and durability of plastic materials.</p></p>
