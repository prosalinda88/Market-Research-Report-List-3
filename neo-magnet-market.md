<p><p>Neo magnets are commonly used in two main market types, bonding and sintering. In the bonding market, neo magnets are made by mixing neodymium, iron, and boron powders with a polymer matrix, resulting in a strong and versatile magnet. In the sintering market, neo magnets are produced through a process of pressing and sintering the powdered materials, creating a more durable and high-performing magnet. Both market types cater to different industries and applications, providing unique benefits and characteristics.</p></p>
