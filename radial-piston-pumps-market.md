<p><p>Radial Piston Pumps come in two main market types: Single-stage Pump and Multi-stage Pump. A Single-stage Pump consists of a single rotating assembly with pistons that move radially to create pressure and move fluid. On the other hand, a Multi-stage Pump contains multiple rotating assemblies stacked on top of each other, allowing for higher pressures and more fluid movement. Both types are important in various industries such as automotive, construction, and manufacturing for their ability to efficiently pump fluids.</p></p>
