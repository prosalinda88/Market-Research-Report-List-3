<p><p>Silicon transformer oil market can be classified into two main types; Low Hydrogen Silicone Oil and High Hydrogen Silicone Oil. Low Hydrogen Silicone Oil, also known as low molecular weight silicone oil, is commonly used in applications where electrical insulation and heat transfer properties are crucial. On the other hand, High Hydrogen Silicone Oil, or high molecular weight silicone oil, offers improved stability and viscosity under high temperatures, making it suitable for more demanding applications in transformers and electrical systems.</p></p>
