<p><p>The audio D-A converter market is segmented into four types: R-2R, string, high-speed current-steering, and delta-sigma converters. R-2R converters use a resistor ladder network to convert digital signals to analog. String converters use a string of resistors in a ladder network. High-speed current-steering converters operate at high speeds using current steering techniques. Delta-sigma converters oversample the input signal and use delta-sigma modulation to achieve high resolution. These market segments are expected to experience growth and demand from 2022 to 2028.</p></p>
