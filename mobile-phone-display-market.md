<p><p>The mobile phone display market consists of two main types of technologies - LCD and OLED. LCD displays use liquid crystals to create images with backlighting, offering a cost-effective solution with good picture quality. On the other hand, OLED displays use organic light-emitting diodes to produce vibrant colors and higher contrast ratios without the need for backlighting, resulting in thinner and more energy-efficient screens. Both technologies cater to different consumer needs and preferences in the mobile phone market.</p></p>
