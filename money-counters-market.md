<p><p>Money counters are available in three main market types - None, UV and MG, UV, MG, and IR, and UV, WM, and MG. The None option is a basic counter without any counterfeit detection features. UV and MG counters use ultraviolet and magnetic ink detection to identify fake bills. UV, MG, and IR counters add infrared technology to enhance counterfeit detection. Lastly, UV, WM, and MG counters include watermark detection in addition to ultraviolet and magnetic ink detection for increased security.</p></p>
