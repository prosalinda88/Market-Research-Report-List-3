<p><p>The natural gas pipeline system market consists of three main types - gathering pipelines, transportation pipelines, and distribution pipelines. Gathering pipelines gather natural gas from production wells and transport it to processing plants or main transmission pipelines. Transportation pipelines move natural gas long distances across states or countries. Distribution pipelines deliver natural gas directly to homes, businesses, and other end users. Each type plays a crucial role in ensuring the efficient and reliable delivery of natural gas to consumers.</p></p>
