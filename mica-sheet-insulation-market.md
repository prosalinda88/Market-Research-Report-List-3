<p><p>Mica Sheet Insulation Market includes two main types: Rigid Mica Sheet and Flexible Mica Sheet. Rigid Mica Sheets are stiff and durable, ideal for high-temperature applications where stability is crucial. Flexible Mica Sheets, on the other hand, are more pliable and can be easily bent or curved to fit irregular surfaces. They are commonly used in applications where flexibility is required, such as in electrical insulation for motors and generators. Both types of Mica Sheet offer excellent thermal and electrical insulation properties.</p></p>
