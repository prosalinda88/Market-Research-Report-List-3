<p><p>FD&C dyes are widely used in various industries, including food, cosmetics, pharmaceuticals, and textiles. The market for FD&C dyes can be classified into three main types: powder dyes, granule dyes, and liquid dyes. Powder dyes are finely ground particles that are easily soluble in liquid formulations. Granule dyes consist of larger particles that are suitable for applications where uniform dispersion is required. Liquid dyes are pre-dissolved in a liquid medium, making them ready to use in various products without additional processing.</p></p>
