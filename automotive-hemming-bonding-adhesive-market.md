<p><p>The automotive hemming bonding adhesive market can be categorized into two types: one-component and two-component adhesive. One-component adhesive is pre-mixed and ready to use, requiring no additional mixing or preparation before application. On the other hand, two-component adhesive consists of two separate components that are mixed together before application, allowing for precise control over curing time and bond strength. Each type of adhesive offers unique advantages and is chosen based on specific application requirements in the automotive industry.</p></p>
