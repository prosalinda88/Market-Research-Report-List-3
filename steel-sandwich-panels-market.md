<p><p>Steel sandwich panels are lightweight construction materials that consist of an insulating core sandwiched between two layers of steel sheet. They are widely used in the construction industry for roofing, wall cladding, and partition walls in residential, commercial, and industrial buildings.</p><p>The Steel Sandwich Panels Market is experiencing significant growth due to the increasing demand for energy-efficient and sustainable building materials.
