<p><strong>The Terahertz Spectroscopy System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Terahertz Time Domain Spectroscopy</li><li>Terahertz Frequency Domain Spectroscopy</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Terahertz spectroscopy systems are categorized into two main types: Terahertz Time Domain Spectroscopy (THz-TDS) and Terahertz Frequency Domain Spectroscopy (THz-FDS). In THz-TDS, the system emits short pulses of terahertz radiation and measures the time it takes for these pulses to reflect off a sample, providing information on the material's properties. In THz-FDS, the system analyzes the frequency of terahertz waves absorbed or reflected by a sample, allowing for detailed analysis of the sample's composition and structure. Both techniques offer valuable insights for various scientific and industrial applications.</p></p>
