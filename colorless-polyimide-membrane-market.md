<p><strong>The Colorless Polyimide Membrane Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Biphenyl Type Polyimide Film</li><li>Homobenzene Polyimide Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Colorless polyimide membrane market is segmented into two types: Biphenyl type polyimide film and homobenzene polyimide film. Biphenyl type polyimide film is made with biphenyl dianhydride and aromatic diamine, offering high thermal stability and excellent mechanical properties. Homobenzene polyimide film, on the other hand, is produced using homobenzene dianhydride and diamine, providing superior optical clarity and electrical insulation. Both types are widely used in various industries such as electronics, aerospace, and automotive for their unique properties.</p></p>
