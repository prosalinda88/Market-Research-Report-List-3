<p><p>Epitaxial equipment market includes various types such as Metal Organic Chemical Vapor Deposition (MOCVD) equipment, Molecular Beam Epitaxy (MBE) equipment, and Other Chemical Vapor Deposition (CVD) devices. MOCVD equipment is used for growing epitaxial layers on a substrate, MBE equipment involves depositing atoms on a heated substrate in a high vacuum environment, and Other CVD devices are utilized for growing thin films on semiconductor materials. These technologies play a crucial role in the production of advanced semiconductor devices and optoelectronic components.</p></p>
