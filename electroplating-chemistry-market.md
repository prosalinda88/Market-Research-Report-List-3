<p><p>Electroplating chemistry market consists of three main types of products: Pretreatment agents are used to clean and prepare the surface of the substrate before electroplating. Electroplating additives are chemicals added to the plating bath to enhance the quality and efficiency of the plating process. Post-treatment agents are applied after plating to improve the final finish, durability, and corrosion resistance of the plated surface. These products play a crucial role in achieving high-quality and long-lasting electroplated products.</p></p>
