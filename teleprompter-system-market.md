<p><p>Camera Mounted Teleprompter refers to a teleprompter system that is attached directly to the camera lens, allowing the presenter to read the script while looking directly into the camera. Floor or Stand Teleprompter, on the other hand, is a standalone device placed on the floor or attached to a stand, allowing the presenter to read the script while looking slightly off-camera. Other types of teleprompter systems include speech prompters, presidential teleprompters, and tablet teleprompters, each designed for specific applications and settings.</p></p>
