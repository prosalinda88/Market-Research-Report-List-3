<p><p>Timber wrap films refer to packaging material used to protect timber during storage and transportation. There are three main market types of timber wrap films - HDPE wrap films, LDPE wrap films, and PP wrap films. HDPE wrap films are known for their high strength and tear resistance, LDPE wrap films are more flexible and provide good sealing properties, while PP wrap films offer excellent moisture resistance and durability. Each type of film caters to different needs and preferences in the timber packaging industry.</p></p>
