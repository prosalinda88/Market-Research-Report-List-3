Colorless to colored materials change from a transparent or colorless state to a colored state when subjected to heat, while colored to colorless materials change from a colored state to a colorless state under the influence of heat. Both types of materials are used in various applications such as smart windows, sensors, and consumer goods for their unique color-changing properties.</p></p>
