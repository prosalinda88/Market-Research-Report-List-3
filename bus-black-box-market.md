<p><p>The Bus Black Box market consists of two main types: Integrated and Portable. Integrated black boxes are permanently installed within the bus and seamlessly integrate with the vehicle's systems to capture data on a continuous basis. Portable black boxes, on the other hand, are standalone devices that can be easily installed and removed from the bus as needed. Both types serve the same purpose of recording and analyzing important data related to the bus's operation and performance.</p></p>
