<p><p>Medical external defibrillators are divided into two main types: semi-automatic defibrillators and automatic defibrillators. </p><p>Semi-automatic defibrillators require the user to manually push a shock button after analyzing the heart rhythm, while automatic defibrillators deliver a shock automatically without the need for user intervention.</p><p>Both types of defibrillators are essential in treating sudden cardiac arrest by delivering an electric shock to restore the heart's normal rhythm. They are widely used in hospitals, ambulances, and public spaces to help save lives in emergency situations.</p></p>
