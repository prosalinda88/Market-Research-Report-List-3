<p><p>Aluminum roof coatings are a type of coating applied to roofs to protect them from weathering and extend their lifespan. <p><p>Aluminum roof coatings come in two main types: fiber and non-fiber. Fiber aluminum roof coatings contain added fibers to increase durability and strength, making them ideal for roofs that experience heavy foot traffic. Non-fiber aluminum roof coatings, on the other hand, do not contain any added fibers and are typically used for roofs that do not require as much strength. Both types provide excellent protection against UV rays and weathering, helping to extend the life of the roof.</p></p>
