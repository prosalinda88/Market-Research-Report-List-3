<p><p>Rapid food safety test kits are available in two main types - immunoassay test and hybrid test. Immunoassay tests detect the presence of specific molecules in a sample using antibodies, providing quick results for common contaminants like pathogens and allergens. Hybrid tests combine different technologies to offer a more comprehensive analysis, including DNA detection for microbial identification. Both types of tests are crucial for ensuring the safety and quality of food products, especially in industries where rapid and accurate testing is essential.</p></p>
