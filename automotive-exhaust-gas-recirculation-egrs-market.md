<p><p>Automotive Exhaust Gas Recirculation (EGR) systems come in two main types: Pneumatic EGR Valve and Electric EGR Valve. Pneumatic EGR valves operate based on pressure differentials, using air pressure to control the recirculation of exhaust gases. On the other hand, Electric EGR valves use an electric motor to regulate the flow of exhaust gases. Both types play a crucial role in reducing emissions and improving fuel efficiency in vehicles by recirculating a portion of exhaust gases back into the engine for combustion.</p></p>
