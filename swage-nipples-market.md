<p><p>Swage nipples are available in two main market types: concentric and eccentric. Concentric swage nipples have both ends aligned and are commonly used for connecting pipes of the same diameter. Eccentric swage nipples have one end slightly off-center, making them ideal for connecting pipes of different diameters or for situations where proper alignment is required. Both types are widely used in various industries such as oil and gas, chemical, and construction for their versatility in pipe fittings and connections.</p></p>
