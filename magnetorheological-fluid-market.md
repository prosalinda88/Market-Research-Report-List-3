<p><p>Magnetorheological fluid is a type of smart fluid that can change its viscosity when subjected to a magnetic field. It is composed of small magnetic particles suspended in a carrier fluid, such as oil or water. When a magnetic field is applied, the particles align themselves and increase the overall viscosity of the fluid. This unique property makes magnetorheological fluids ideal for various industrial applications, such as dampers, shock absorbers, and clutches, where precise control of viscosity is required.</p><p>The Magnetorheological Fluid Market is witnessing significant growth due to the increasing demand for smart materials in various industries.
