<p><p>Heat resistant Polymethyl Methacrylate market consists of two main types- Extrusion Grade and Injection Grade. Extrusion Grade PMMA is primarily used in processes where the material is forced through a shaped die to create products such as tubing or sheeting. Both grades offer heat resistance properties, making them suitable for applications requiring high-temperature endurance.</p></p>
