<p><p>Membrane Electrode Assemblies (MEA) are essential components in proton exchange membrane fuel cells, with different types catering to specific applications. The 3-layer MEA consists of a catalyst layer sandwiched between the electrode and proton exchange membrane, offering high performance and durability. The 5-layer MEA adds gas diffusion layers for improved gas flow and water management. Other market variations include advanced MEAs with additional layers or modifications tailored to enhance specific fuel cell functions and meet varying industry requirements.</p></p>
